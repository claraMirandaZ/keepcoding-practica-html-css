Luna Lovegood

About me

Hey there...! My name is Luna Lovegood and I am a witch. Only daughter of Xenophilius and Pandora Lovegood, my mother accidentally died while experimenting with spells when I was nine and thus I was raised by my father, who's the editor of a magazine called The Quibbler. I live in a rook-like house near the village of Ottery St. Catchpole in Devonshire, England, but attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. There, I was sorted into Ravenclaw House, whose members are characterised by their wit, learning, and wisdom.

During my school years, I met Harry Potter and I know at first he found me rather odd, but was grateful that I was one of the few people to believe his story that Lord Voldemort had returned. He really sympathised with the loss my mother and my status as an outcast.

In my fourth year I became an important member of Dumbledore's Army and participated in the Battle of the Department of Mysteries and the Battle of the Astronomy Tower. I co-led the reconstituted Dumbledore's Army when Hogwarts fell under the control of Lord Voldemort with Severus Snape as headmaster, while Harry Potter, Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley where away during their Horcrux-seeking journey.

Because of my father's political dissidence at the time, I was abducted by Death Eaters to be held ransom, and imprisoned in the dungeons of Malfoy Manor for months until I could return to Hogwarts to participate in the final battle of the Second Wizarding War.

Now that school is over and Voldemort has been defeated, I'm eager for the future. I've grown to like magizoology so maybe that's where I'll be headed towards...!
